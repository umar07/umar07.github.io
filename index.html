Umar Masud

Hi 👋! I am a researcher and developer in the field of AI/ML.
Currently, I am a Member of Technical Staff at Cerebras where I contribute to Model Integration and Quality Engineering with a focus on pre-deployment testing/validation of LLMs including evals, performance, API, etc.

I graduated (MSc) from the University of Toronto 🍁 and interned at Samsung AI Center Toronto, working with the camera technology team. Prior to that, I did my undergrad in Computer Engineering at JMI University, New Delhi. During my undergrad, I also spent a couple of months in Ulm University, Germany, doing academic research. 🇩🇪

I have a strong background in AI, with a focus on multimedia data. My work includes top-conference paper submissions (CVPR 26' & ECCV 22' workshops), experience with large-scale data (millions), prototyping ML models and understanding the complete ML lifecycle. I have done many personal projects and academic internships in the ML domain, solving a variety of problems. Currently exploring internals of modern LLM inference and software. 🚀

My interest is in making machines understand vision & language as we humans do.
I love to make and own end-to-end projects that solve real-life problems.

Additionally, I am enthusiastic about innovation, ideas, entrepreneurship, community, and the like. A keen interest in the start-up ecosystem.
